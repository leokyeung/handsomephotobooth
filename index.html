Frequently Asked Questions

How does the booking process work?

+

Please complete the form below, and we’ll send you a final quote. If you’d like to proceed, we’ll provide an agreement to sign. A 50% deposit will be due to secure your date, and you’ll receive a confirmation email with all booking details.

Do you offer additional hours services

+

Yes, we do!

What props come with the package?

+

We offer a wide variety of props, including hats, signs, and funny glasses. We keep our selection updated frequently to match current trends, which is one of the reasons many people choose to book with us.

Do you provide setup and breakdown?

+

Yes! Our team handles all setup and breakdown. We typically arrive 1 hour before your event starts for setup and will stay for breakdown after your rental period ends.

Can we customize the photobooth templates?

+

Absolutely! Our talented designer creates personalized templates that match your event's theme and style. We offer a variety of pre designed templates or can create completely custom designs.

What areas do you serve?

+

We serve the entire San Francisco Bay Area including San Francisco, Oakland, San Jose, and surrounding cities. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our standard service area. Contact us for specific location details.
