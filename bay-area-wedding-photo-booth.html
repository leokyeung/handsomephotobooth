Questions couples ask before booking

A few quick answers so you know what to expect before you reach out about availability.

What makes a good wedding photo booth?

Choose a setup with a professional DSLR camera, studio flash umbrellas, and high-speed dye-sublimation printers for flattering photos and dry, smudge-free prints.

How early should we book?

Once your venue and wedding date are confirmed, it is worth checking availability. Popular spring, summer, and fall Bay Area dates book 4–9 months out.

Can the template match our wedding?

Yes. Your print template can include your names, wedding date, colors, florals, typography, and overall wedding style.

Do guests get digital copies?

Yes. Guests can use QR downloads during the reception, and you will receive a full digital album after the wedding.

How much space does the photo booth need?

Plan for roughly an 10 ft x 10 ft footprint for the booth, backdrop, and prop table. We are flexible if your venue is tight on space, just inform us during booking.

Do you need power or Wi-Fi at the venue?

We need one standard power outlet within reach of the setup. Wi-Fi is helpful for QR downloads, but we can also use a mobile hotspot when venue Wi-Fi is limited.

Is a deposit required?

Yes. A 50% deposit secures your wedding date. The remaining balance is due 30 days before the event.

Open-air or enclosed booth?

We use an open-air style setup, which fits group photos better and looks more polished in your wedding photos and on Instagram.

Ready to see if your wedding date is open?

Most Bay Area wedding dates book 4–9 months out. Tell us yours and we will reply within 12 hours with availability and a final quote.
